About

Hey there! I’m Harley, a 19-year-old student developer based in the Philippines with a passion for technology, design, and gaming. I thrive on creating sleek and intuitive user experiences, blending creativity with logic to bring ideas to life. As a bilingual learner, I’m currently adding Japanese to my skill set, constantly seeking ways to expand my horizons.

When it comes to programming, I’ve gained experience in languages like C#, MySQL, HTML, CSS, Java, and JavaScript. My focus is on building better interfaces that truly connect users with the digital world. I’m currently studying Information Technology, where I’m constantly refining my skills, learning new technologies, and pushing myself to stay ahead of the curve.

Outside of coding, I love diving into graphic design, gaming, and exploring the world of cars and audio setups. These hobbies not only fuel my creativity but also sharpen my problem-solving skills. I’m excited to keep growing, exploring new challenges, and seeing where my passion for tech and design takes me in the future!

As an individual, I have a wide range of hobbies and interests that showcase my technical and creative abilities. As a software developer, I enjoy building software applications, experimenting with new technologies and continuously developing my skills. I am also passionate about electrical and hardware maintenance and repairs, which allows me to tinker with various electronic gadgets and gain a deeper understanding of how things work.

In my free time, I enjoy playing badminton, engaging in car-related activities such as modifications and maintenance, and playing games on my favorite console. I have a fascination for old hardware, and I love exploring and collecting vintage technology. I also have a passion for audio and keyboards, which includes tinkering with sound equipment and experimenting with different types of keyboards.

I am also interested in photography and cinematography, which allows me to capture and express my creativity visually. As a digital designer, I enjoy creating visual designs that engage and captivate the audience. Finally, I also enjoy console homebrew and jailbreaking, which allows me to customize my devices and push the boundaries of what is possible.
